how an engagement works

Gap to audit-ready. No hand-waving.

Every engagement follows the same structure. Assess first, implement second, document last. The SSP reflects what's actually in place - not what someone wishes were in place.

01

Gap Analysis

Evaluate your current environment against all 110 NIST SP 800-171 controls. Identify what's missing, what's partially implemented, and what's already done. Delivered as a report with your SPRS score.

02

SSP + POA&M

System Security Plan documenting your environment and how each control is implemented. Plan of Action and Milestones for every gap, with remediation timelines.

03

Technical Remediation

Implement the missing controls - CUI enclaves, access restrictions, encryption, logging, MFA, configuration baselines. Terraform and Ansible where it makes sense. Documented as it's built.

04

Evidence & Retainer

Collect and organize the 300-500 evidence artifacts a C3PAO will want to see. Ongoing monthly retainer to maintain compliance, catch drift, and keep your SPRS score accurate.
