assessment
CMMC Gap Assessment
Starting at $5,000
Scope determines final cost.

Evaluate your current environment against all 110 NIST SP 800-171 controls. Delivered as a detailed report with:

  • Current SPRS score
  • Control-by-control status (implemented / partially / missing)
  • SSP outline
  • POA&M with prioritized remediation items
  • Scoping analysis - what's in scope and what isn't

What you walk away with is a roadmap. Not a checkbox. Something you can hand to a C3PAO and say "this is where we are."

implementation
Technical Remediation
Pricing Project-based.
Scoped after gap analysis.

Gap analysis delivered the map. This is the work of actually closing the gaps. That includes:

  • CUI enclave architecture (AWS, on-prem, or hybrid)
  • Access control configuration and least-privilege IAM
  • Audit logging to CMMC AU domain requirements
  • Encryption at rest and in transit
  • MFA enforcement across CUI-touching systems
  • Configuration baselines (Terraform/Ansible where applicable)
  • Document version control and change log implementation

Most consultants write a policy and stop. The policy is useless without the technical implementation behind it.
